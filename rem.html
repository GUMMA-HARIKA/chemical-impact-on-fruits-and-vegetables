Pest Control Soil Fertility Watering Tips

Natural Pest Control

  • Use neem oil spray to deter harmful insects.
  • Plant companion plants like marigolds to repel pests.
  • Introduce beneficial insects like ladybugs and praying mantises.
  • Use homemade garlic or chili sprays to ward off pests.

Enhancing Soil Fertility

  • Compost kitchen waste to create nutrient-rich organic compost.
  • Use natural fertilizers like manure or bone meal.
  • Practice crop rotation to maintain soil health.
  • Use mulch to retain moisture and add organic matter to the soil.

Watering Tips

  • Water plants early in the morning to reduce evaporation.
  • Use drip irrigation for efficient water usage.
  • Avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.
  • Collect rainwater for sustainable irrigation.
